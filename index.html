The Afterdream is a realm of non-existence where all the lost, abandoned, and forgotten things find themselves. Explore this comedically depressing realm with your friends in a new tabletop roleplaying experience.

Play as over 30+ pre-defined Character Types or make up your own! Fend off hostile lost souls, vengeful neglected houseplants, disembodied voices, ghost trains, and even sentient smells. A brand new violence-optional roleplaying game in the works with SO many more surprises in store!
